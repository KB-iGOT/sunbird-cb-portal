In-Service Training Programme (IST)

Department of Personnel and Training in collaboration with Civil Services Training Institutions/ Management/ Academic Institutions/ organizations offers In-service Training 5 days offline and 3 days online training programs for All India Service (IAS, IPS & IFoS) Officers, Officers working under Central Staffing Scheme and Officers of Central Secretariat Service (CSS)/Central Secretariat Stenographers Service (CSSS) (Officers of the level of DS/Sr.PPS and above). These programs aim to address the competency requirements of civil servants at mid and senior management level. They provide an opportunity to the officers to enhance their skills and develop a deeper appreciation of national priorities and goals  

Eligibility:

Officers eligible to apply :

AIS (IAS, IPS IFoS) officers with minimum 4 years of service (as on April 1, 2023) and up to 2018 batch (upto the ranking of Secretary/ Chief Secretary).

Group ‘A’ Officers working under Central Staffing Scheme of DoPT.

Officers of the level of DS/Sr. PPS and above of Central Secretariat Service (CSS) and Central Secretariat Stenographer Service (CSSS).

An Officer can attend only one In-service training programme during IST calendar.

Officers superannuating in the month of training are not epgible for the program scheduled in that month.

Officers may not apply for training program which they have already attended during the last 3 years (2020-21, 2021-22 and 2022-23)
